Sentiment Analysis of UN Security Council Resolutions


My sister was recently describing her thesis on the progression of womans rights as portrayed by advancements in UN Security Council Resolutions and so I decided to run a program to anylyse the average "happy-tone" of each piece and graphed these values against the year the piece was written. To provide context, she has illustrated this graph to describe what each turn means.


Above is a graph produced from the resultant data.
